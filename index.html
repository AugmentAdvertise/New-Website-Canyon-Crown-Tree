About Us

Salt Lake City's Most Trusted Arborists

Founded in 2010 by Alex Rivera, an ISA-Certified Arborist with over 15 years of experience, Canyon Crown Tree has grown into one of the Wasatch Front's most trusted tree care companies. We combine deep knowledge of Utah's native and ornamental tree species with industry-leading equipment and safety protocols.

Our team of certified professionals is committed to preserving the health and beauty of your trees while ensuring the safety of your property. Every job we take on reflects our core values: expertise, integrity, and environmental stewardship.
