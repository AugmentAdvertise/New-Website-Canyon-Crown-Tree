Comprehensive Tree Care Solutions

Canyon Crown Tree offers a full range of professional tree services for residential and commercial properties throughout Salt Lake City and Northern Utah.

Tree Removal

Safe removal of hazardous, dead, or unwanted trees from your property. Our certified arborists use industry-leading techniques and equipment to handle removals of all sizes with precision and care.

  • Hazardous tree assessment
  • Crane-assisted removals
  • Confined space rigging
  • Complete debris cleanup
  • Wood recycling and chip delivery
  • Permit assistance when needed

Pruning & Trimming

Expert pruning to improve tree health, appearance, and safety. Our arborists use science-based techniques following ANSI A300 standards to ensure proper cuts that promote strong, healthy growth.

  • Crown thinning and cleaning
  • Crown raising and reduction
  • Deadwood removal
  • Structural pruning
  • Vista pruning for views
  • Fruit tree pruning
Learn More

Stump Grinding

Complete stump removal to reclaim your yard space. Our commercial-grade grinders handle stumps of any size, grinding them 6-12 inches below grade so you can replant, resod, or landscape the area.

  • Stumps ground 6-12 inches below grade
  • Any size stump handled
  • Root grinding available
  • Chips cleaned up or left as mulch
  • Surface roots addressed
  • Area ready for replanting

How It Works

Our straightforward process ensures you receive expert tree care with complete transparency from start to finish.

1

Free Consultation

Contact us by phone or online. We'll schedule a convenient time for one of our certified arborists to visit your property at no cost or obligation.

2

Expert Assessment

Your arborist will evaluate your trees, discuss your goals, explain all options, and provide a detailed, transparent written estimate on the spot.

3

Professional Service

Our trained crew arrives on schedule with the right equipment. We follow ISA best practices and OSHA safety standards on every single job.

4

Complete Cleanup

We remove all debris, rake the area, and leave your property cleaner than we found it. All wood and green waste is recycled responsibly.
