Rapid Response When It Matters Most

Utah's Wasatch Front is no stranger to severe weather. From the heavy, wet spring snowstorms that snap branches under the weight to the powerful summer microbursts that can topple entire trees, storm events create dangerous situations that demand immediate professional attention. Canyon Crown Tree maintains a dedicated emergency response crew available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

When you call our emergency line at (801) 555-9111, you speak directly with an on-call arborist—not a voicemail, not an answering service. We assess the severity of your situation over the phone, dispatch a crew, and arrive on-site prepared to stabilize, secure, and resolve the hazard. For true emergencies involving occupied structures, blocked roadways, or proximity to power lines, our typical response time is 1 to 2 hours within the Salt Lake City metro area.

Our emergency services address a wide range of critical situations: trees that have fallen on homes, garages, or vehicles; large limbs hanging precariously in the canopy; uprooted trees leaning against structures or fences; wind-thrown trees blocking driveways and roads; and trees entangled with power lines. We work closely with Rocky Mountain Power and local utility crews when energized lines are involved, ensuring the scene is safe before we begin any removal work.

Important: If a tree has brought down power lines or is in contact with electrical wires, stay at least 35 feet away and call Rocky Mountain Power at 1-888-221-7070 before calling us.

We also understand the stress of dealing with property damage. That is why we provide thorough documentation including time-stamped photographs, a written damage assessment, and itemized invoices formatted specifically for homeowner's insurance claims. We are happy to communicate directly with your insurance adjuster to streamline the claims process and help you recover the cost of emergency tree work.

Emergency Response Process

From your first call to full resolution, here is how we handle tree emergencies.

1

Call Our Emergency Line

Call (801) 555-9111 at any hour. Our on-call arborist assesses the situation, provides safety guidance, and dispatches a crew immediately for critical hazards.

2

On-Site Assessment

Our crew arrives, evaluates structural damage, checks for electrical hazards, and photographs the scene for insurance documentation before beginning any work.

3

Stabilize & Remove

Using advanced rigging and crane equipment when needed, we safely remove the tree or hazardous limbs, protect your property, and tarp any structural openings.

4

Cleanup & Documentation

We clear all debris, provide a complete damage assessment report with photographs, and deliver claim-ready invoices to help you file with your insurance carrier.
