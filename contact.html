Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency tree services, 365 days a year. Whether a storm has brought down a tree on your home, a large limb is threatening your roof, or a tree is leaning dangerously after high winds, our emergency crews are ready to respond. Call our dedicated emergency line at (801) 555-9111 for immediate assistance. We prioritize calls involving structures, roadway blockages, and power line hazards. We typically schedule free estimates within 2 to 3 business days of your initial call, though availability can vary depending on the season. During our busier months (spring and after major storms), wait times may be slightly longer. For urgent situations involving safety hazards, we can often provide same-day or next-day assessments. Once the estimate is approved, most jobs are completed within 1 to 2 weeks, and we will work with your schedule to find a convenient time.
